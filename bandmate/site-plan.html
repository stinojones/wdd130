Content

Home page

The Home page will show navigation to the different pages of taking lessons for different instruments and finding different musicians to jam with or make a band with. It'll have images of different instruments to inspire them to learn more instruments or play with others.

Images for the Home page

Learn page

This page will have a list of different instruments that you can choose from and to the side of them will be a list of different teachers that you can contact with different informoation that they may have or links to there websites. It could also be links to different youtube videos as well to learn.

Images for the Page 2

Jam page

In the Jam page you will be able to find different musicians that are near you or who are willing to jam. You will be able to contact them and know what kind of instrument they play and how long they've been playing it. You will learn their availability and their intentions whether that be to just jam, get better at the instrument, or even start a band.
